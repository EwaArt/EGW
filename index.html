About the Artist

Ewa Gadnicka Wlodarczyk

I am an artist who finds joy in the play of colors, the tranquility of rural landscapes, and the intricate beauty of traditional Polish architecture. My journey in art began with a masterâ€™s degree from the Krakow Academy of Fine Arts, Faculty of Graphics, where I specialized in Lithography and Poster Design in 1987. My career spans graphic design, painting, drawing, pastels, and watercolors, with a special focus on sacred themes and canonical icons. I also conduct workshops in painting and watercolor and organize plein air painting events. My work has been featured in various group and solo exhibitions and is part of private collections in countries such as Poland, Germany, Austria, the United States, Canada, Mexico, and South Africa. I am an active member of the Association of Polish Artists and Designers, a member of the board, and the chair of the audit committee of the Association of Polish Pastelists. Continuously exploring new avenues in art and creativity, I am always thrilled to share my work through multiple art exhibitions, both locally and internationally.
